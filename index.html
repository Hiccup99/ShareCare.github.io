How It Works

Where Home Care Meets Home Share

  • Step 1

    Elders in need of a service list their available living spaces on our catalog along with its location, price, and list of services required.

  • Step 2

    College students or young adults seeking cheaper or free rent create a profile and browse through our catalog of available homes, carefully reviewing the list of required services to perform and send a request to connect when they find a living space suitable for them.

  • Step 3

    After a request for a connection is made, the elder reviews the list of requests for their living space and decides who to move forward with. Contact is contained within the ShareCare application to provide maximum security.

  • Step 4

    Once the pair concludes it is a good match, they move onto the next step and sign a housing agreement through ShareCare, with all payments going through ShareCare as well.
