How to be a ShareCare host



  • Step 1

    Create your listing for free. You can list your listing with ShareCare at no cost. You will have to describe your home, the living space that you are willing to offer, and the types of housekeeping services you would like to have help with. We also ask for you to provide photos and details of the living space.

  • Step 2

    Accept student requests You get to know potential student guests before they can officially book your listing. You are in control of which student you would like to share your own living space with by being able to accept or decline their message and booking requests. We advise that all communication be done through the ShareCare platform to ensure readability, trust and security. You can also greet potential students in person.

  • Step 3

    Select student Choose the right student guest to live in your home and help with specific tasks. ShareCare is based on trust and safety. All student profiles and identities are verified by our service. All requests, questions, and concerns are continued through ShareCare and your account on our website.
